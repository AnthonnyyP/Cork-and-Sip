ABOUT US

Cork & Sip was founded on June 15th, 2023 from three young ambitious individuals by the names of Micole Atkins, Tony Phan, and James Aubrey. The trio teamed up to create an app, because they all shared a love for wine. The vision of the app was to give their audience a classic experience by signing up and creating your own personal "cellar" (as what they would call it). Each guest will have the ability to book their desired wine tasting across the Napa Valley. Also they'll be able to add their favorite wines to their cellar while learning a brief a history on their choice. Cork & Sip is the future and the owners take great pride in producing an app for wine enthusiast across California.
